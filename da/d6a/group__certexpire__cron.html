Create a certexpire_cron instance.

The cron string takes numeric arguments only, but supports ranges (1-5) and selections (1,3,5), or a combination, space separated: minute hour day month weekday minute, 0-59 hour, 0-23 day, 1-31 month, 1-12 weekday, 0-7 (0 == 7 == sunday) man crontab(5) for details.
