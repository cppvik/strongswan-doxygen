ipsec_sa_t*(* ipsec_sa_mgr_t::checkout_by_reqid)(ipsec_sa_mgr_t *this, u_int32_t reqid, bool inbound)

Checkout an installed IPsec SA by its reqid and inbound/outbound flag. Can be used to find the correct SA for an outbound packet.

The matching SA is locked until it is checked in using checkin(). If the matching SA is already checked out, this call blocks until the SA is checked in.

Since other threads may be waiting for a checked out SA, it should be checked in as soon as possible after use.

Parameters
reqidreqid of the SA
inboundTRUE for an inbound SA, FALSE for an outbound SA
Returns
the matching IPsec SA, or NULL if none is found
