enum narrow_hook_t

Kind of narrow hook.

There is a non-authenticated (IKE_AUTH) and a authenticated (CREATE_CHILD_SA) narrowing hook for the initiator. Only one of these hooks is invoked before the exchange. To verify the traffic selectors negotiated, each PRE hook has a POST counterpart that follows. POST hooks are invoked with an authenticated peer. It is usually not a good idea to narrow in the POST hooks, as the resulting traffic selector is not negotiated and results in non-matching policies.
