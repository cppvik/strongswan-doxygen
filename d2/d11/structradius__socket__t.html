radius_message_t*(* radius_socket_t::request)(radius_socket_t *this, radius_message_t *request)

Send a RADIUS request, wait for response.

The socket fills in RADIUS Message identifier, builds a Request-Authenticator and calculates the Message-Authenticator attribute. The received response gets verified using the Response-Identifier and the Message-Authenticator attribute.
