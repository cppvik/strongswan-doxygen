ENCR_AES_GCM_ICV16
Definition: crypter.h:52

From McGrew & Viega http://csrc.nist.gov/groups/ST/toolkit/BCM/documents/proposedmodes/gcm/gcm-revised-spec.pdf Formatted to match our API which expects the first four bytes (salt) of the IV as part of the key and writes/expects the ICV at the end of the cipher text. Since our implementations are currently limited to IV lengths of 12 (IV=8, SALT=4 as per RFC 4106/5282) the test cases 5/6, 11/12 and 17/18 aren't compatible.
