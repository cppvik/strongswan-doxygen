Detailed Description

An object of type ike_sa_id_t is used to identify an IKE_SA.

An IKE_SA is identified by its initiator and responder SPIs. Additionally, it contains the major IKE version of the IKE_SA and, for IKEv2, the role of the daemon (original initiator or responder).
