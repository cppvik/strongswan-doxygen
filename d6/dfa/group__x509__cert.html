va_list  args 
)

Generate a X.509 certificate.

To issue a self-signed certificate, the function takes: BUILD_SUBJECT, BUILD_SUBJECT_ALTNAMES, BUILD_SIGNING_KEY, BUILD_X509_FLAG, BUILD_NOT_BEFORE_TIME, BUILD_NOT_AFTER_TIME, BUILD_SERIAL, BUILD_DIGEST_ALG. To issue certificates from a CA, additionally pass: BUILD_SIGNING_CERT and BUILD_PUBLIC_KEY.
