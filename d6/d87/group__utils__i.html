This macro can be used to dispatch a macro call based on the number of given arguments, for instance:

#define MY_MACRO(...) VA_ARGS_DISPATCH(MY_MACRO, __VA_ARGS__)(__VA_ARGS__)
#define MY_MACRO1(arg) one_arg(arg)
#define MY_MACRO2(arg1,arg2) two_args(arg1,arg2)

MY_MACRO() can now be called with either one or two arguments, which will resolve to one_arg(arg) or two_args(arg1,arg2), respectively.
